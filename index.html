About Cube Worlds

Cube Worlds is an exciting 3D sandbox game that puts you in control of a world made entirely of cubes. Whether you're a creative builder or an adventurous explorer, this game offers endless possibilities for fun and creativity.

How to Play

Controls

  • WASD - Move
  • Mouse - Look around
  • Left Click - Place block
  • Right Click - Remove block

Tips & Tricks

  • Start with simple structures before attempting complex builds
  • Use different block types for variety in your creations
  • Explore the world to find rare materials
